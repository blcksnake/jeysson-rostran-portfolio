About Emma Clarke

Emma Clarke is an award-winning investigative journalist with over 12 years of experience covering politics, corporate accountability, and social justice issues. Her work has appeared in major publications including The New York Times, The Guardian, and The Washington Post.

Known for her meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Emma has broken numerous high-profile stories that have led to policy changes, criminal investigations, and corporate reforms. Her dedication to uncovering the truth has earned her recognition from the journalism community and beyond.

Emma holds a Master's degree in Journalism from Columbia University and a Bachelor's in Political Science from Yale. When not investigating stories, she teaches journalism ethics at NYU and mentors young reporters.
