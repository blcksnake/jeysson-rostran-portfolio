About Dr. Amanda Foster

Dr. Amanda Foster is a board-certified interventional cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in cardiovascular medicine. She completed her medical degree at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine, followed by residency in Internal Medicine and fellowship in Cardiology at Massachusetts General Hospital.

Dr. Foster specializes in complex coronary interventions and has performed over 3,000 cardiac catheterization procedures. She is passionate about preventive cardiology and believes in empowering patients through education and personalized care.

She has published over 50 peer-reviewed articles and is actively involved in clinical research focusing on women's heart health and innovative treatment approaches.
