Isabella Moreau

Born in Paris and trained at the prestigious École de la Chambre Syndicale, I have spent over a decade crafting designs that celebrate the feminine form while pushing the boundaries of contemporary fashion.

My work has been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and Elle, and worn by celebrities on red carpets around the world. I believe fashion should empower, inspire, and tell a story that resonates with each woman who wears my designs.

Based between Paris and New York, I continue to explore new materials, techniques, and sustainable practices that honor both our craft's heritage and our planet's future.
