About the Artist

I'm Elena Martinez, a contemporary artist based in Barcelona. My work explores the intersection of color, emotion, and human experience.

With over a decade of professional experience, I've developed a distinctive style that blends abstract expressionism with figurative elements.

My paintings have been exhibited in galleries across Europe and are part of private collections worldwide.
